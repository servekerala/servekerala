Last updated: December 2025

Welcome to ServeKerala.in (“we”, “us”, “our”). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding that information when you use our website and services.

When you list your business, contact a business, or sign up for services on ServeKerala.in, you may voluntarily provide us with personal data such as your name, phone number, email address, business details, and location information.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our site, we may automatically collect technical data such as your IP address, device type, browser type, referral pages, geographic location, and usage patterns through cookies and similar tracking technologies.

We may use the information we collect for purposes including:

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (including Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager) to personalize your experience, analyze site usage, and track events such as business card clicks.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may impact the functionality of the site.

We may work with third-party service providers such as analytics and marketing tools, payment processors, and hosting providers. These parties may have access to your data only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information:

  • When required for safety, security, or legal reasons.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You can choose not to provide certain information; however, that may limit your ability to use certain features of the site. You may also request access to, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below.
